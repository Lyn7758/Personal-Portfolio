Research

"A Discrete Model for Bike Share Inventory" is a paper that presents a mathematical model for optimizing the inventory management of bike-sharing systems. The model considers the number of bikes at different locations and the demand for bikes at these locations over time. The goal of the model is to minimize the total cost of moving bikes from one location to another while ensuring that there is always an adequate supply of bikes at each location to meet demand. The paper provides a mathematical formulation of the problem and presents numerical results to demonstrate the effectiveness of the proposed model.
